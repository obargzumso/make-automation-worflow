# Tools & Services Required

## Make.com
- Free plan available
- Sign up at: https://make.com

## Google Sheets
- Google account required
- Create a sheet with columns: Timestamp, Name, Email, Message

## Webhook
- Generated automatically in Make.com
- Paste URL into your form's action field

## Gmail / SMTP
- Connect your Gmail account in Make.com
- Or use any SMTP provider
